💼 Freelancing Starter Guide

🚀 How to Get Your First Client

  1. Build a portfolio - 3-5 solid projects
  2. Create profiles on Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn
  3. Network locally - Tell friends, family, local businesses
  4. Offer free work for testimonials (limit: 1-2 projects)
  5. Cold email small businesses with bad websites
  6. Post on social media - Show your work on Twitter/X, LinkedIn

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Starting work without a contract
  • ❌ Not taking a deposit (50% upfront)
  • ❌ Underpricing your work
  • ❌ Scope creep (client keeps asking for more)
  • ❌ Working without clear deadlines
